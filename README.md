# Chocolate Sales Performance

## Project Overview
The Chocolate Sales Analysis project was developed to evaluate overall business performance, identify revenue trends, analyse product demand, and assess sales team effectiveness.

## Objectives
•	Identify growth trends\
•	Track sales performance\
•	Monitor product demand\
•	Monitor overall sales performance\
•	Evaluate sales team efficiency

## Workflow
### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation
• Fixed inconsistencies and formatting\
• Handled missing values logically\
• Create relationships between tables
### 2️⃣ KPI & Exploratory Analysis
#### Calculated:
•	YOY Growth\
•	Prior Sales\
•	Total Sales\
•	Month to Date Sales\
•	Quarter to Date Sales
#### Analise Revenue by:
•	Tracks total sales and number of boxes shipped over time.\
•	Visual geographic breakdown.\
•	Monthly and weekly performance distribution\
•	Top-performing products for production

#### Row-Level Security (RLS) Implementation

•	Salespersons analyse only personal performance\
•	Managers evaluate team results.\
•	Executives monitor overall company performance.

## Conclusion
The Chocolate Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance across multiple dimensions—time, geography, product, and personnel.
Despite achieving substantial total revenue, the negative YOY growth signals the need for strategic intervention. The insights generated through this dashboard enable data-driven decisions that can improve profitability, operational efficiency, and long-term growth.
